Summer Camp ☀️ July • Outdoor & online

Coddy Summer Camp

Age 10–17 • Prishtina & Tirana

A full week of coding, games and mini‑hackathons. Students learn web, games and basic AI while building projects they can proudly show at home.

Winter Camp ❄️ December–January

Coddy Winter Camp

Age 10–17 • Cozy coding labs

During the winter holidays, students dive into intensive coding sessions, warming up with hot chocolate and cool projects — from mini‑apps to simple games.

Python basics Logic & problem solving Code & creativity
